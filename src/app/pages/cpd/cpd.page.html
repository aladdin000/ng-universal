CPD cycles

After becoming certified, you’ll be assigned a CPD cycle and expected to earn 40 credits every two years throughout your career. The CPD program is based on 24-month cycles and credits may be earned by participating in a wide range of learning activities. Participation in the program is optional for FRMs and mandatory for ERPs.
