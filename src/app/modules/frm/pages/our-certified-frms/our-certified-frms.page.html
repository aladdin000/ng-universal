Continuing Professional Development

Risk management is an evolving discipline with new challenges emerging every day. These challenges make continued learning essential for FRMs. Our CPD program delivers a broad range of accessible learning opportunities and provides the tools you need to track and document your activities. FRMs and ERPs are expected to actively participate throughout their careers.

GARP Membership

Addressing risk is a team effort, and with over 200,000 members in more than 190 countries and territories, GARP members are making a difference. GARP membership provides the risk community with engagement opportunities and a platform to have a voice in industry research. It also offers access to unparalleled networking opportunities, exclusive risk intelligence content, and much, much more. FRMs and ERPs receive special rates.
