Become a Chapter Director

We rely on the dedication and enthusiasm of volunteer Chapter Directors to raise GARP awareness and develop a network of risk professionals looking to advance their knowledge and careers. Email chapters@garp.com for Director opportunities. Please include your chapter location.

Volunteer in a supporting role

Many Chapter Directors seek the support of their fellow members in the planning and execution of meetings, either individually or through the formation of a committee. Whether by identifying topics, recruiting speakers, scoping out meeting spaces, or even greeting attendees at the door, you can contribute to your chapter's success by participating as a volunteer.

Email chapters@garp.com for volunteer opportunities. Please include your chapter location.
