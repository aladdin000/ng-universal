What is the GARP Buy Side Risk Managers Forum?

The Buy Side Risk Managers Forum was formed in 2003 as an informal organization to bring together senior risk professionals from some of the largest asset management firms and the investment management subsidiaries of banks and insurance firms to discuss current and emerging risks on the Buy Side. Since that time, the Forum has met on an average of twice-yearly to discuss and work on developing risk management best practices related to the types of firms represented by the Forum's membership. The Forum also co-developed the "Risk Principles for Asset Managers" with Capital Market Risk Advisors, LLC in 2008.

GARP Buy Side Risk Managers Forum membership is by invitation-only and is limited to heads of risk management, chief risk officers, or their equivalents positioned at large buy side firms with assets under management generally in the top quintile of the industry.

Background

In May 2013, the Forum approached GARP about an affiliation. GARP's position as the world's leading risk management professional association and the Forum's desire to expand its activities and outreach to a more global audience, presented a strong and mutually beneficial value proposition to both groups. In June 2013 GARP's Board of Trustees unanimously voted to organize the GARP Buy Side Risk Managers Forum and to formally transfer the Forum's activities to GARP for the ultimate benefit of its global membership.

Purpose of Buy Side Risk Managers Forum

The Forum's purpose is to identify and explore issues of interest to large buy side firms while looking to delineate and publicize thought leadership and best practices. The GARP Buy Side Risk Managers Forum will organize special interest subcommittees to research specific issues and report to the full group on their findings at its semi-annual meetings, at which time the Forum's membership will debate and review the subcommittee's conclusions with the possible objective of preparing educational assets to be made available for GARP's global membership.

Additionally, and in furtherance of its mission, the GARP Buy Side Risk Managers Forum's membership will serve to connect buy-side risk professionals from around the world in a non-biased, non-sales-related learning environment.
